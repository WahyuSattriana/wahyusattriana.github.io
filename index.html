I DESIGN SINCE 4TH GRADE

The first design software I used was CorelDraw X4. When I finished making something there, I felt so satisfied with it. Then my brother introduced me to another editing software named Adobe Photoshop. I was so confused by the tools that Photoshop has. And yes, CorelDraw and Photoshop have different usability. I tried editing some photos in Photoshop while watching several tutorials, but I still preferred designing in CorelDraw. From there, Adobe Illustrator appeared in front of my face. My desire to design is back. So I kept learning design graphics, even until I was in middle school. I learned some design basics, such as color and font combinations, layouts, and others. In middle school, I pushed myself to brand my name as a designer to my friends, and because of it, I succeed in making a bit of a penny even my teacher trusted me to handle my school's Instagram. THEN, STARTED FILMING

I love spending my time watching YouTube in 2016. That was the golden era of Indonesia's YouTube content. I've seen many YouTubers adding cinematic clips to their content. So from that, I'm willing to do it by myself. I started recording anything I like. Recording myself gaming (uhh cringe), editing tutorials, and my favorite, making cinematic videos. Many people see me like what the hell am I doing with my camera phone. But unfortunately, I was just a 5th-grade kid so I don't care. Filmmaking has a ton of aspects to learn like color grading, sound design, storytelling, and a bunch of technical stuff. In high school me with friends joining a small online video contest and rewarded the top 10 by the judge's choice.
